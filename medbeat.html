Overview

MedBeat is an mobile application concept that is meant to help transfer students with their anxiety and depression throughout their transfer journey using music and AI to create personalized playlists

Brainstorming

During this stage, the group collaborated to come up with the different websites that we wanted to consider with our redesign before we narrowed down to WebReg. Once we determined to focus on WebReg, we then started to consider some of the main issues that might appear and that we want to focus on.

Surveying

The group created a survey to see what transfer students mainly use music for and what kind of emotions that they go through when they are preparing for transfer and/or after they already transfered.

Survey Analysis

After receiving responses to our survey, our group analyzed the responses to see the commonalities to see what are the major points that we needed to focus on. Interviews were also conducted after the mockups were made to make changes to our prototype to better match what our population would be looking for or are interested in.
