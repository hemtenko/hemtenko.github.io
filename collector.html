Overview

The Collectors developed an application to help those that collect blindbox figures from companies such as PopMart and Sonny Angels to be able to keep track of their current collection as well as view what other figures that they might be missing or want to obtain. The application also contains a way for users to buy and trade figures with other users.

Requirements

Our team determined the top 10 requirements that were needed for our business and our application. After narrowing down to those 10 requirements, those were then narrowed down to the top 5 most important requirements. Those requirements were then broken down into different components and tasks to visualize what needs to be done to meet those requirements as well as include descriptions of each requirement and their importance.
