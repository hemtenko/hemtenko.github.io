Hi! I'm Hamilton Ko

I am a Taiwanese-American designer, born and raised in Southern California.

I am currently a senior at the University of California, Irvine and am pursuing a degree in Informatics with a focus in Human-Computer Interactions.

I first began getting into design from my early love of Legos. Growing up, I was always intrigued by technology and grew up using devices such as computers and tablets. What always intrigued me about technology was how their designs, whether through applications or through physical machines, worked alongside with what people do in their daily lives. As a designer, I strive to learn more about and implement designs that are focused on being integrated in everyday lives while also providing ease of use and accessibility.

In my free time, I like to dance and improve my skills through making photo edits and poster designs.
