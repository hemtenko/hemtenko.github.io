Uniwallet

Our team, Design Dazzlers, developed a budgeting mobile application to help students keep track of their spendings and maintain their budget with the use of AI, creating an easier budgeting experience.

Overview

Team Members: Jason Lee, Andy Dien, Axel Dejesus Lopez, and Cecilia O'Grady

Research

Competitve Analysis Report

Our team conducted a competitive analysis to access what features that other similar applications had to ensure that the most important features are included while also observing what can be inlcuded to make our application stand out from the competitors.

Surveying

Survey Analysis

An online survey was created and distributed to students that receive financial aid to obtain information that would help determine what our group would need to focus on while developing the application. After answers were collected, an analysis was done to decide what concerns that needed to be addressed as well as what features were mandatory. This allowed for general trends and opinons to be reviewed.
