Overview

Our team, Design Dazzlers, developed a budgeting mobile application to help students keep track of their spendings and maintain their budget with the use of AI, creating an easier and educative budgeting experience.

Research

Our team conducted a competitive analysis to access what features that other similar applications had to ensure that the most important features were included while also observing what can other features could be inlcuded to make our application stand out from the competitors.

Surveying

Survey Analysis

An online survey was created and distributed to our target population, college students that receive financial aid, to obtain information that would help determine what concerns were present and needed to be addressed. This helped up to determine what features were important for us to focus on and develop. This also allowed for our group to see the general trends and options that emergred from our target population.
