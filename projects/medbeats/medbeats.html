MedBeat

Group Members: Hamilton Ko (Team Lead), Irvin Zarate (Project Lead), Leticia Morales-Ruiz (UX Lead), Danny Lin (CTO)

Overview

MedBeat is an mobile application concept that is meant to help transfer students with their anxiety and depression throughout their transfer journey using music and AI to create personalized playlists

Brainstorming

During this stage, the group collaborated to come up with the different websites that we wanted to consider with our redesign before we narrowed down to WebReg. Once we determined to focus on WebReg, we then started to consider some of the main issues that might appear and that we want to focus on.

Surveying

The group created a survey to see what transfer students mainly use music for and what kind of emotions that they go through when they are preparing for transfer and/or after they already transfered. These responses were then analyzed to see the commonalities that would make up the main points that we would focus on.
