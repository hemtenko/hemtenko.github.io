WebReg: Redesigned

Team Members: Giovanna Kim-Van Mancinelli, Hamilton Ko, Andy Dien, Eric Liu, and Ronica Cheng

Our group, Informagics, decided to redesign WebReg to create a better user experience as well as make the website be more accessible for students to use when enrolling in classes.

Brainstorming

During this stage, the group collaborated to come up with the different websites that we wanted to consider with our redesign before we narrowed down to WebReg. Once we determined to focus on WebReg, we then started to consider some of the main issues that might appear and that we want to focus on.

Interviewing

Interview Protocol Document

Before we started to interview people in relation to WebReg, our group first came up with a list of questions that all participants would answer along with a protocol to ensure that each interview is being conduct the same way.

Expert Analysis

Expert Analysis Report

After conducting our interviews, our group started to conduct additional research to learn more about the website and the features that were present and available to the user. Then, we determined which features still needed to be present in the redesign as well as what features needed to be redone.

Prototyping

Prototype Protocol and Notes

After conducting our research and going through the results of our interviews, our group used that information to start creating mockups and then an interactive prototype for users to test out and gain more feedback on.

Final Report
